CHOCOLATE BROWNIES


Your go-to spot for the best homemade brownies! Dive into the joy of baking with our easy-to-follow recipe that guarantees rich, chewy goodness.

Ingrediends


  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar

  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour

  • 2/3 cup cocoa powder

  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar

  • 1/2 cup dark chocolate chips

  • 3/4 teaspoons salt

  • 2 eggs

  • 1/2 cup oil

  • 2 tablespoons water

  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla


Instructions


  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).

  2. Combine the sugar, flour, powdered sugar, cocoa powder, chocolate chips, and salt in a medium bowl.

  3. Whisk together the eggs, olive oil, and water in a large one.

  4. Sprinkle the dry mixture over the wet one, and fold until just combined.

  5. Pour the batter into a baking pan lined with parchment paper.

  6. Bake for 40 to 45 minutes, until a toothpick inserted comes out with a few crumbs attached.

  7. Remove from the oven and let it cool before serving.
